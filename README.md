# Seeing Accessibility
The Jupyter notebook details a pipeline to score the quality of pedestrian infrastructure in supporting inclusive mobility, using a vision-language approach on street view imagery. 
The notebook demonstrates the application of the pipeline in Liverpool, UK, where the prevalence of disabled population and level of deprivationa are higher than national averages.

In the notebook, we also map the spatial inequality in infrastructure quality with disability vulnerability and deprivation to identify priority areas for accessibility intervention, and reveal areas where existing outcome-based deprivation indices such as the Index of Multiple Deprivation (IMD) fall short in identifying accessibility vulnerability.
